Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and provide personalized content. Here's what you need to know about our use of cookies:

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To improve the performance and reliability of our website
  • To analyze how our website is used and improve our services
  • To personalize your experience on our website

Types of cookies we use

We use both session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

Your choices regarding cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

More information

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
