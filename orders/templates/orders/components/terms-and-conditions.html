Delivery Terms and Conditions
1. Delivery Area and Times

We currently offer delivery services exclusively within Westerstede (26655). Delivery times are between 9 AM and 6 PM, Monday through Saturday.

2. Order Placement and Cutoff Times

Orders placed before 4 PM on weekdays will be delivered the same day. Orders placed after 4 PM or on weekends will be delivered the next business day.

3. Product Quality and Freshness

We guarantee the freshness of all products at the time of delivery. If you're not satisfied with the quality, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a replacement or refund.

4. Missed Deliveries

If you're not available to receive your order, we will attempt to contact you. If unsuccessful, we'll leave the order in a safe place or return it to our facility, depending on the perishability of the items.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

Your personal information is protected under our Privacy Policy. We use your data solely for order processing and delivery purposes.
